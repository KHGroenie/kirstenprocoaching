About Kirsten

Meet Kirsten

"Motion is lotion."

With over 10 years of coaching experience and a background in biokinetics, I bridge the gap between high-performance training and sustainable, pain-free movement.

My approach is grounded in practicality and science. Whether you're an ultra-runner aiming for a PR, a cyclist looking for more power, or a desk worker wanting to play with your kids without back pain, the fundamental truth remains the same: how you move dictates how you live.

I don't believe in quick fixes. I believe in consistent, intelligent functional strength training that respects your body's mechanics and elevates your everyday life.
